# **App Name**: Smart Pet Care

## Core Features:

- Personalized Pet Profiles: Users can create and manage detailed profiles for each pet, including name, type, breed, age, weight, vaccination history, and uploaded medical reports. This information is stored and used for tailored recommendations.
- AI Pet Symptom Analyzer: Utilize an AI tool to analyze photos or short videos uploaded by users to detect potential pet health issues like skin infections or wounds, and provide simple explanations, suggested care steps, and direct access to finding nearby veterinarians.
- Direct Vet Connection: Easily connect with veterinary professionals through one-click calls, in-app chat functionalities, or streamlined appointment booking functionality.
- Automated Health Reminders: Receive timely notifications for essential pet care activities, such as vaccination dates, feeding times, medication schedules, and upcoming vet checkups.
- Emergency Aid & Vet Finder: A prominent 'Emergency Button' provides immediate access to first-aid instructions, displays the nearest animal hospitals, and allows one-click calling to emergency veterinary services.
- Voice-Guided Navigation: Support for voice commands in multiple languages (English, Hindi, Odia) allows users to interact with an AI tool that interprets requests and provides simple, spoken responses, making navigation and information retrieval accessible for all users.
- Comprehensive Health Log: Maintain a secure history of all AI scans, uploaded medical records, treatments, and vet visits, allowing users to track their pet's health progression over time.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: A calming and reliable muted blue (#40A2BF), selected to evoke trustworthiness and care, ensuring excellent visibility and contrast on a light interface.
- Background color: A very light, highly desaturated blue (#ECF2F4), providing a clean, airy, and accessible foundation that prioritizes maximum readability for all user segments, including the elderly.
- Accent color: A soft, uplifting lavender-blue (#939EDD) to gently highlight interactive elements and guide user attention. This analogous hue is distinct from the primary but maintains a cohesive, non-distracting palette.
- Font family: 'PT Sans' (humanist sans-serif) for all text elements, chosen specifically for its outstanding legibility, clear character definition, and inviting nature, which supports both headlines and essential information in a user-friendly manner.
- Employ large, intuitively designed, and universally recognizable line-art icons. Icons will be central to navigation and understanding, minimizing reliance on text and ensuring ease of use for users with limited smartphone experience.
- A simplified, grid-based layout with generous whitespace to prevent visual clutter. Key interactive elements are presented as large, prominent buttons with high contrast, designed for easy identification and tapping.
- Incorporate subtle, quick, and purposeful animations for user feedback and seamless transitions. Animations are designed to enhance user comprehension and flow without being distracting or contributing to a perception of slowness.
